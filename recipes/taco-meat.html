Taco Meat

Description

Taco Meat.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ½ teaspoon garlic salt
  • ½ teaspoon celery salt
  • ½ teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 (8 ounce) can tomato sauce, or more to taste

Steps

  1. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat. Cook and stir beef in the hot
    skillet until browned and crumbly, 5 to 7 minutes.
  2. Season beef with onion powder, garlic salt, celery salt, and cumin. Pour
    tomato sauce over the beef, stir to coat, and simmer until thickened, slightly,
    about 5 minutes.
